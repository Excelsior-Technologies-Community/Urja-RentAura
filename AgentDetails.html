About

I am a multi-lingual agent who can speak English, Chinese, Hindi, Punjabi, and Urdu. I was born and raised in India, then moved to Canada to further my academic career in Hospitality and Culinary. I have always been fascinated by the real estate industry, and when I decided that real estate was the right career for me, I jumped in with both feet and never looked back. The home buying process can be frustrating and confusing, especially for first-time buyers, but I take pride in making my clients feel comfortable at all stages of the process. With constant communication and the highest commitment to service, I am known for my authenticity, timely responses, and enthusiasm. When I am not helping families buy/sell homes, I enjoy table tennis, movies, anything technology-related, and spending time with my loved ones and my two Yorkies, Cheeku and Kiwi.
