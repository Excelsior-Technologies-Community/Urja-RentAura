About Us

Mexico Property Search is an online real estate marketplace with more than 20 years of experience in the housing industry, featuring residential and commercial listings.

Over 20+ Years Experience
in Real Estate Services

Whether you're interested in buying a home, seeking apartments for rent, searching for office space or looking to invest in commercial properties, Mexico Property Search is the place to start.
Plus, if you're a real estate agent, we can help boost your business by efficiently advertising your properties on our platform. That's because Mexico Property Search generates more than 90 million visits per year and is present on multiple markets. Headquartered in Saskatoon, we operate in Canada, the United States and several international locations, including Mexico, Puerto Rico, Costa Rica, the Dominican Republic and more.
